About Charlie

Hi there! My name is Charlie, and I'm an 18-year-old tutor passionate about helping kids learn English and Chinese in a way that's fun, engaging, and effective.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, but I've been living in Taiwan for the past 12 years. This unique experience has given me a deep understanding of both cultures and languages, and I love sharing that knowledge with others.

My teaching style is like a big brother - it's all about making learning an adventure! I believe that kids learn best when they're having fun, so I incorporate a variety of creative activities into my lessons, including:

Creative Learning Activities

Cardboard Crafts: We build amazing structures, create characters, and learn new vocabulary through hands-on projects.

Origami: Folding paper into beautiful shapes is a fantastic way to practice fine motor skills and learn about different cultures.

Coding with Minecraft Education Edition: We build worlds, solve puzzles, and learn coding concepts while having a blast in the Minecraft universe.

Gaming with Roblox and Minecraft: We play games, complete challenges, and build our language skills through interactive gameplay.

Music & Learning

But that's not all! I'm also a musician who loves to play the piano, ukulele, and guitar. Music is a powerful tool for learning, and I often incorporate it into my lessons to help kids learn new words, phrases, and even grammar concepts.

Think of me as your big brother who's here to help you unlock your language potential. I'm patient, enthusiastic, and always ready to make learning a positive experience.
